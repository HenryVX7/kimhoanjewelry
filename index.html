Our Story

Since 1972, we've proudly served the Phoenix community. Kim Hoan Jewelry has been more than just a store—it’s been a tangible showcase of our pursuit of the American Dream. Our family emigrated from Vietnam with a dream to bring elegant, meaningful jewelry to our new home. Through decades of dedication and cultural pride, we’ve become a name people trust.
