Our Services

We offer custom jewelry design, resizing, polishing, and repair services. Experience bespoke craftsmanship with a personal touch.

Cleaning & Inspection

At Kim Hoan Jewelry, we understand that your precious pieces deserve exceptional care. Our professional jewelry cleaning service is designed to restore the brilliance of your gold, diamond, and gemstone items. Using gentle, industry-approved techniques, we remove built-up dirt, oils, and residue that can dull the natural beauty of your jewelry. Whether it’s an heirloom ring or your favorite daily necklace, we handle each piece with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring it leaves our store sparkling like new.

In addition to cleaning, our expert maintenance service keeps your jewelry in top condition for years to come. From tightening loose stones and checking prongs to polishing metals and re-plating white gold, we offer comprehensive care that extends the life of your treasured items. Regular maintenance not only protects your investment but also enhances its appearance and comfort. Trust Kim Hoan Jewelry to preserve the elegance and craftsmanship of your jewelry with every visit.

Fitting & Customization

At Kim Hoan Jewelry, we believe that every piece of jewelry should fit perfectly—both in size and style. Our fitting service ensures that your rings, bracelets, necklaces, and other fine pieces are tailored precisely to your comfort. Whether you need a ring resized or a chain adjusted for daily wear, our skilled jewelers handle each adjustment with precision and care, maintaining the integrity and craftsmanship of every item. With personalized fitting, we make sure your jewelry doesn’t just look beautiful—it feels beautiful too.

In addition to expert fitting, we specialize in bespoke customization to bring your unique vision to life. Whether you’re designing an engagement ring from scratch or adding a personal touch to an existing piece, our customization service offers endless possibilities. Choose from a curated selection of precious metals, gemstones, and design elements, and collaborate with our artisans to create something truly one-of-a-kind. At Kim Hoan Jewelry, we transform your ideas into elegant, lasting expressions of individuality and love.
