Our History

Welcome to Kim Hoan Jewelry. We are a long standing, family-owned jewelry business that specializes in gold and jade. We originally emigrated from Vietnam and have lived in Phoenix since 1972.
Since then, we have served the Phoenix community with jewelry for all budgets alongside services like cleanings and sizing consultations.
